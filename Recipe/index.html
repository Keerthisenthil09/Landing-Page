Idli and Vada

Idli and vada, a delightful South Indian duo, where soft idlis meet crispy vadas, offering a symphony of flavor and texture. It makes te best combo for breakfast

Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa, a savory South Indian delicacy, featuring a thin, crispy crepe filled with a spiced potato filling, served with coconut chutney and sambar for a burst of flavors.

Ven Pongal

Ven Pongal, a comforting South Indian dish made from rice and lentils, cooked to creamy perfection with mild spices, making it a hearty and wholesome breakfast or lunch option.

Sambar

Sambar, a flavorful South Indian lentil stew made with a tantalizing blend of spices, vegetables, and tangy tamarind, perfect to pair with rice, idli, dosa, or vada for a satisfying meal.

Milagu Rasam

Milagu Rasam, a comforting South Indian soup bursting with the flavors of black pepper, cumin, and garlic, simmered in a tamarind-infused broth, providing warmth and healing with every spoonful.

Chow Chow Kootu

Chow Chow Kootu, a comforting South Indian dish featuring chayote squash cooked with lentils and aromatic spices.

Carrot Curry

Carrot Curry, a delightful South Indian dish, marrying the sweetness of carrots with the aromatic spices of curry leaves and coconut.

Ghee Rice

Ghee Rice, Fragrant and flavorful, this South Indian delicacy combines aromatic basmati rice with the richness of clarified butter for a truly indulgent experience.
